Welcome to the exceptional natural setting of Les Orres ski resort in the Hautes-Alpes
From its ski area which peaks above Serre-Ponçon Lake, admire the panoramic views over the Ecrins National Park on one side and the Parpaillon mountain range on the other, whizz down the slopes lined by larch forests and enjoy the sunshine of the Southern Alps.
